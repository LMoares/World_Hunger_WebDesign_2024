The Zero Hunger Project

Our Directive


At Zero Hunger, our mission is to ensure that noone in our supervised communities goes hungry. Through the assistance of people like you, we are able to operate Food Banks, Donation Drives, and Volunteer Programs that help those in need. In addition, by partnering with local communities, we're able to identify areas where food scarcity exists and dedicate resources there.

Here at Zero Hunger we strive to erradicate hunger from every community around the globe. To facilitate that immense ambition, we require the assistance of members in those communities to donate their time and resources to help their neighbors in need.

There are several ways you can help your fellow man. At Zero Hunger, our operations are always in need of those willing to dedicate their time. Consider Volunteering at your local Food Drive or Consider Donating to help with a monetary pledge and have your pledge go directly to assisting your local community and beyond!
